Questions

Frequently Asked

How should guidance be understood?

This guidance is traditional, cultural, and educational. It is meant to help clients understand possible Vastu, Numerology, color, direction, layout, and remedy options. It does not replace medical, legal, financial, architectural, or professional advice.

Is this only for one religion?

No. Vastu and Numerology guidance is available for clients from all backgrounds. The consultation focuses on space, direction, colors, numbers, layout, and practical guidance. It is traditional and educational, not limited to one religion.

Is a floor plan required?

Yes, a floor plan or simple room sketch is strongly recommended for Vastu. Photos and entrance direction are also helpful.

Can Numerology be done online?

Yes. Numerology can be completed online using the client's full name, date of birth, business name if needed, and main concern.

Do remedies require major construction?

Not always. Many remedies may involve placement changes, colors, crystals, direction adjustments, or simple corrections. Major changes are not always required.
